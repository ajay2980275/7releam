It's the Little Things: Living your Life in the Seven Realms

With the nature of chronic pain, we often miss noticing the little things that we enjoy every day and make us feel good. Simply the process of writing down these positive affirmations can often be transformational since it reinforces all of the activities you have been doing in the past months. This is the concept of neuroplasticity. You can change your brain and help yourself and others feel good at the same time! Why not continue what you learned.

 

Be a little creative and make a list of the little things that touch your life every day and make you feel better in each realm of your life. The seven realms (BLESS ME) include the body (physical), lifestyle (behaviors), emotions (feelings), society (people), spirit (beliefs), mind (thoughts), and environment (surroundings). Refer to the Seven Realms chart to remind you of the risk factors, protective factors, and small actions you can take every day to feel better with more energy.

 

Review the examples of my little things list that make each of us feel good each day. Post your little things list on the website for others to see and use as an example.
