Emotionally Empty to Emotionally Ecstatic: Solutions to overcome emotion barriers

We all have negative emotions or moods such as anxiety, fear, anger, and depression that linger longer than we want. There are many activities that can pull us out of these situations such as creative activities, helping others and even exercise. It is first important to be mindful of your emotions throughout the day and know how to change them. Here are some solutions to the barriers that often come up. Overcoming these barriers will help you learn to change negative emotions into positive emotions. Click on any barrier brick to find solutions to break down the emotional barriers. Changes from negative to positive emotions are easiest when you develop a routine of healthy habits of creative activities that excite you each day. Take pauses throughout the day to check-in on your emotions. Practice calming to shift to more positive emotions. Are you ready to LEAP into healthier pain-free emotions?
