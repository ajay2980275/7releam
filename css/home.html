Welcome to the Personalized Activated Care and Training (PACT)

Self-Management Training to Prevent and Relieve Chronic Pain and Addiction

Chronic pain is the big elephant in the room of health care. It is the top reason for health care, disability, and addiction. More than half of the persons who have pain conditions at 1 month still have pain 5 years later despite treatment. The major reason is the lack of self-management training to help people reduce the many lifestyle risk factors that lead to delayed recovery and chronic pain. The PACT can help you solve this problem.
