Health Coaching to Support Your Success

 
 
Health Coach
 
 

About Health Coaching

In the PACT program, health coaching is designed to a relationship-centered, client-driven process designed to facilitate and support you in making changes and achieve your goals inpreventing chronic pain and addiction while promoting yourhealth and well-being. Here is how you can use the health coach to support your success in this program.

 

Goals of the Health Coach

In the PACT program, the health coach will help you:

Set goals and provide you with support inachieving them

Review interest, readiness for change, understanding, barriers, and action plans

Facilitate change using a safe, consistent, non-judgmental,and supportive approach

Empower your own choicesto engage in self-care with your intrinsic self-motivation and energy

Engaging responsibly to inspire and support you to functionindependently and successfully

Engagingyour internal strengths and external resources to achieve sustainable changes in your thoughts, emotions, and lifestyle choices

 

Selecting Your Health Coach

After enrolling in the PACT program, you can select your own personal health coach among a list of coaches under their tab

By selecting your health coach, an email will be sent to him or her to make an introduction

The health coach will respond to you with an email to explain the process

 

The First Visit

At the first visit, the health coach will call you

Together, you will decide how the coach can best help you improve your condition

You need to establish goals and work on how best to achieve them

Your coach will provide a safe, consistent, non-judgmental, and supportive approach at each visit

Together, you can both set up a series of additional calls in your personal PACT calendar

 

Scheduling Additional Calls

You are provided eight calls initially will but can add more as needed

At each call, you can discuss the program, its goals, your action plan, and how you can best achieve them

You can both track your progress in the dashboard, any barriers that arise, and how best to overcome them

You can also send emails and comments directly to the health coach or to the PACT coaching leadership. These comments can be added to the health coach comments section
