Welcome to Personalized Activated Care and Training (PACT)

 

Self-Management Training to Prevent and Relieve Chronic Pain

Chronic pain is the big elephant in the room of healthcare. It is the top reason for seeking care, disability, and addiction. More than half of all people who have experienced pain conditions at 1 month still have pain 5 years later despite treatment. The major reason for this is the lack of self-management training to help people reduce the many lifestyle risk factors that lead to delayed recovery and chronic pain. The PACT program can help you solve this problem.
