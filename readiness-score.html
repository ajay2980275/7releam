Readiness to Change Score

 
 
 

Readiness to Change Score 7.6

 
Guide to Interpretation

Precontemplation (0 to 3) –

You are interested in change but may not be ready to make any changes quite yet.

Contemplation (3 to 5) –

You are considering your goals and what changes you would like to make and the barriers that need to be addressed to accomplish them.

Preparation (5 to 7) –

You are prepared to change and are willing to make the changes and address the barriers to create how you want to be.

Action (7 to 10) –

You are ready to learn the new skills and lifestyles necessary to create how you want to be.
