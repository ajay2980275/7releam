Privacy Policy

 

This privacy policy (the “privacy policy”) covers the collection, use and disclosure of the personal data of any Fellow (as defined in the Terms of use) using SRS’s web-based platform (the “platform”) or SRS’s mobile application (the “SRS Application”).

Your privacy is important to SRS. SRS, and any service provider that may operate the Platform or the SRS Application on behalf of SRS, will use all reasonable measures to keep your personal data secure. By using the platform or the SRS Application and submitting personal data through the platform or the SRS Application, you agree to the collection, processing and storing of your data in accordance with this Privacy and SRS Application.

This Privacy policy forms an integral part of the Terms of Use of the Platform and the SRS Application.

a) Cookies

Like many websites, SRS uses cookies to collect visitor information, for example to enable us to identify your device or to remember selected features from a previous session (e.g. through Google Analytics). Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that SRS’s Platform transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your web browser. It is possible to prevent cookies from being used in your browser by turning the feature off. Additionally, SRS enables Facebook to track the visitors’ behavior on the website by the use of Pixels, among other. You may opt out of this pixel tracking by clicking on the following link: http://www.aboutads.info/choices.

b) What information do we collect?

When you register as a Participant Fellow or Fellow onto the Platform or when you post a comment on the Platform, you provide SRS with personal data, which can include your first and last name, date and place of birth, address, email address, phone number, language, profession, education, credit card information and more generally any information that identifies you (your “Personal Data”). You have the right to access and rectify at any time your Personal Data collected from you by SRS.Please contact SRS at the address indicated below in section (f).

c) What do we do with your data?

SRS uses your Personal Data in order to improve and administer the Platform, to create your account, to communicate with you, and more generally to provide Participant Fellows and Fellows with services available through the Platform. SRS will store and process your Personal Data in accordance with this Privacy Policy. SRS must collect and use your Personal Data in order to register you as a Participant Fellow and grant you Partial access to the features of the Platform or as a Fellow and grant you full access to the features of the Platform. SRS may contact you to announce SRS events, or any other SRS-related services.

d) How do we secure your data and when do we disclose it?

SRS implements all appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your Personal Data against accidental or unlawful destruction or accidental loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or access for third parties and against other unlawful forms of processing. These measures will also be implement by any processor of your personal Data authorized by SRS in accordance with this Privacy Policy. You acknowledge and agree that SRS may use and/or disclose your Personal Data if SRS deems that such use and/or disclosure is reasonably necessary to (1) comply with legal process, (2) enforce the Terms of Use of the Platform or the SRS Application or the Terms of Participation to the Platform, or (3) Protect rights, Property or personal safety of SRS, the Users of the platform, or the public, Additionally, SRS may request a third party service provider to collect, store and process your Personal Data on SRS’s behalf (for example to maintain the IT system or to process your credit card information, e.g. when you make a donation). SRS may also transfer the data it collects to third parties within the EU, or outside the EU in compliance with the Safe Harbor requirements. Such data may include your email address. If you do not wish that SRS transfer your data to any third party other than the third party service provider mentioned in the paragraph above, please write an email to info@sevenrealmssolutions.com. SRS will take all appropriate measures to ensure that this third party service provider will use the same level of care and security that SRS uses in relation to your Personal Data.

SRS also uses third-party analytics services to help understand your usage of our services. In particular, SRS provides a limited amount of information (such as your email address and sign-up date, birthdate, gender) to intercom, Inc. (“Intercom”) and utilizes Intercom to collect data for analytics purposes when you visit our Platform. Intercom analyzes your Use of our Platform and/or product and tracks our relationship so that we can improve SRS’s services to you. We may also use intercom as a, medium for communications, either through email, or through messages within our product(s). As part of our service agreements, intercom collects publicly available contact and social information related to you, such as your email address, gender, company, job title, photos, website URL’s, social network handles and physical addresses, to enhance your user experience. For more information on the Privacy practices of Intercom, please visit http://docs.intercom.io/privacy. Intercom’s services are governed by intercom’s terms of use which can be found at http://docs.intercom.io/terms. If you would like to opt out of having this information collected by or submitted to Intercom, please contact us at info@sevenrealmssolutions.com.

e) Patient Information

No Submission (as defined in the Terms of Use) shall contain any personal information concerning a patient, unless this information is anonymous. You are solely responsible for ensuring compliance with this provision, and in no event shall SRS nor any person related to it, be held liable for any loss or damage, whether direct or indirect, that may arise from the Submission of patient data that is not anonymized.

f) Contact

In case of questions, concerns or comments regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing, rectification of, or access to your personal data, please contact us at the following e-mail address: at info@sevenrealmssolutions.com.
